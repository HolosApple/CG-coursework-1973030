Run command: make & ./CornellBox

Default mode is 1->Wire frame
Press 1 for Wireframe Mode
Press 2 for Rasterizer Mode
Press 3 for Raytracer Mode

Press 7 for Jump Animation

For Light Positioning
Press w to move the light up
Press a to move the light left
Press d to move the light right
Press s to move the light down
Press q to move the light forward
Press e to move the light forward

For Camera Positioning
Press the up arrow key to move the camera up
Press <- to move the camera left
Press -> to move the camera right
Press the down arrow key to move the camera down
Press 9 to move the camera forward
Press 0 to move the camera backward
Press x for rotation along the x axis
Press y for rotation along the y axis
Press z for rotation along the z axis
